Wo Long: Fallen Dynasty is Being Review Bombed

Wo Long: Fallen Dynasty is being review bombed on Steam as players report running into numerous issues with the PC version of the game.

Parth Bagaria Published 4 days ago

Wo Long: Fallen Dynasty is being review bombed on Steam as fans complain about numerous issues present in the PC version of the game. Wo Long: Fallen Dynasty is the latest Soulslike game made by Team Ninja, the developers behind other titles in this genre like Stranger of Paradise: Final Fantasy Origin and the Nioh series of games.

Wo Long: Fallen Dynasty has received positive reviews from critics, although its score on Metacritic is lower than that of both Nioh titles. Praise has been directed towards its gameplay and level of challenge, with some comparisons made to 2019’s Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice as well. Despite the overall positive critical reception, it looks like fans are facing some issues with the PC version of Wo Long as users are now leaving negative reviews for the game on Steam.

At the time of this writing, Wo Long: Fallen Dynasty has a rating of “Mostly Negative” on Steam with almost 6,000 user reviews. By comparison, Nioh has a “Mostly Positive” rating on Steam, with its sequel sitting at “Very Positive” on the platform. Judging by the user reviews, it looks like players are facing all sorts of optimization problems with the PC port of Wo Long: Fallen Dynasty, which appears to be the primary reason for them leaving negative ratings. In fact, many users go on to praise the gameplay of Wo Long in the review before sharing their disappointment in the current state of the PC version.

Many fans mention the mouse and keyboard controls being poor, while others claim to have run into frame rate issues while playing Wo Long on PC. Even users with a relatively higher-end gaming PC are reporting problems, so it doesn’t look like the issues are limited to lower-spec machines. The system requirements of Wo Long: Fallen Dynasty don’t appear to be that high either, as a GeForce RTX 2060 or Radeon RX 5700 XT is needed for 60 FPS gameplay at 1080p.

Some players also recommend waiting for the developer to fix the optimization issues in Wo Long: Fallen Dynasty and playing Nioh 2 instead. Nioh and Nioh 2 launched first on PS4 before being ported to PC, but Wo Long: Fallen Dynasty was a simultaneous release on all platforms. So, perhaps the development team needed that extra time to better optimize the PC version of Wo Long: Fallen Dynasty and properly address all the users’ concerns with the port.

Wo Long: Fallen Dynasty is available now for PC, PS4, PS5, Xbox One, and Xbox Series X/S.
